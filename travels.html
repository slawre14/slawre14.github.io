My Travels

United States

I have traveled the east coast of the United States extensively.In the past 8 years, I have visited Boston, New York, Washington D.C, Philadelphia, Baltimore, and Richmond. I loved my experience there because of all the history established in most of these cities. I also really enjoyed visiting a large urbanized city with so much presence from the business world.

Europe

Four years ago, I had the pleasure of visiting Europe for the first time. I went to France, Austria, and Italy and visited Paris, Vienna, Venice, and Florence, among other cities. It was a great experience interacting with people from different backgrounds and the environment was very different in comparison the the U.S. I really enjoyed walking around the cities and touring the various historic sites and land marks.

This summer I will be studying abroad in Madrid, Spain for two and a half months. I hope to improve my Spanish while I am there and learn more about their culture. I am also very excited to travel to other cities in Spain, and Europe at large.
