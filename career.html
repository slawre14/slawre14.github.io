Career

Future Business Woman, Chef, and Entrepreneur

I had always wanted to open my own business at some point in my life. It was not until I got to college, however, that I realized how strong my passion for entrepreneurship was and how dedicated I am to creating an idea.

I want to become a business owner of a restaurant, or at least the dessert and bakery business.

I have always tried to be health conscious,and I want to create a place where people can both enjoy the food they eat without sacrificing the health factor. This year, I had the wonderful opportunity of creating a blog, Enticingly Healthy, which focuses on providing healthy recipes and beneficial resources about eating healthy without sacrificing taste and satisfaction with what you eat. It has been a such a wonderful experience discovering more about myself and my passion for what I love to do- cook.
